Lou Fabrizio

Lou holds an MBA from Nichols College and spent over twenty years in executive marketing and management positions at financial institutions in the Northeast including MultiBank Financial Corp., and Direct Federal Credit Union. His marketing experience of the important role that banking plays in the lives of customers has provided a strong foundation for understanding the opportunities of companies in other categories with a similar strong human connection.

Michael Fortuna

Michael has over thirty years of advertising experience as an agency copywriter/creative director/agency principal. He was a founding partner of Emerson Lane Fortuna, as well as a founding partner of Arnold Fortuna Lane (now Arnold Worldwide), an agency that has morphed into one of the largest in the country. During his agency career, he managed and created campaigns for many New England service companies, including Shawmut Bank, BayBank, Fleet Financial, New England Life and Harvard Pilgrim Healthcare.
